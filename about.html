About Us

The story behind every piece we craft

Our Story

Crafted by hand, worn for a lifetime

Lumière Jewelry began in 2018 as a small workshop in Dhaka, founded on the belief that fine jewelry should be made slowly, deliberately, and to last for generations.

Each piece passes through the hands of a handful of artisans — from wax carving to casting, stone setting, and final polish — before it ever reaches you. We source our gold and gemstones responsibly, working only with suppliers who meet our standards for ethics and quality.

Whether it's an everyday band or a once-in-a-lifetime engagement ring, we pour the same care into every piece we make.

Ethical Sourcing

Every gemstone and gram of gold is traceable to responsible, certified sources.

Handmade Craft

No two pieces leave our atelier exactly alike — each carries the mark of its maker.

Made to Last

Our pieces come with lifetime polishing and resizing, because jewelry should age with you.
